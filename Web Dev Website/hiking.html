Hiking in the Cache Valley Area

Greg Child said it best when he said, "Somewhere between the bottom of the climb and the summit is the answer to the mystery why we climb."

Hiking is a fun and rewarding activity. There are trails to fit almost any desire from short, quick trails just to get out for a lunch break, medium trails to help motivate you to progress to more challenges, and long trails that take you beyond your destination.

I can't say that I have a favorite trail, but among my favorite trails are the Preston Valley, Limber Pine, High Creek Lake, and Wellsville Cone to name a few. You discover places that seem more familiar from a dream or a movie. Living in a high desert range I have found many trails that made me wonder if I was hiking a trail in the lush green forrests of Oregon.
