Rock Climbing in the Cache Valley Area

The rush brought by rock climbing made it an instant hobby.

The Cache Valley area is home of 350+ rock climbing routes from simple bouldering routes to routes greater than or equal to 5.14. Logan Canyon contains many routes right off the road or within a short drive from the canyon. There are also other areas around the valley that contain great climbing routes.

We like to go climbing with friends and as a family. The confidence that it builds in my kids is priceless and as a side benefit, the physical challenge it presents is great. I will never forget the first time we took out 2 boys. Our youngest, being 4 at the time, had no fear except for bugs. He had gotten off the ground about 10 feet when he came across a box-elder bug and declared he was done and promptly kicked back from the wall to belay down.
