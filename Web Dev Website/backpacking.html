Backpacking in the Cache Valley Area

It is always a great break to pack the essentials into your pack and hit the trail.

There are multiple places to go enjoy and see the beauty that surrounds you in the mountains. One aspect of backpacking is being able to throw down your tent or pad wherever you'd like and most of the time you will never be bothered by neighbors, with the exception of natural visitors of course.
