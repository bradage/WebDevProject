Outdoor Fun in the Cache Valley Area

There are few places that have as many fun exciting things to offer outdoors as the Cache Valley area. Nestled in the quiet mountains of Northern Utah and South-Eastern Idaho at 4,469 feet, Cache Valley offers many fun trails for hiking, backpacking, rock climbing and so much more.

Whether you wish to soak up some warm sunlight, enjoy the coolness of shaded trails and climbing areas, explore and get away from the daily grind or experience sites that one can only obtain by being outdoors come explore some of the great places I have ventured to with family and friends.
